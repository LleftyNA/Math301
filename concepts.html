Main Characters:

The characters have a charming and doll-like look to them, which is supposed to make the viewer feel more empathy toward them and their stories.

Iceboy Draft:

Low poly models with bright and glowy surface make them stand out against the background of a dry, polluted and destroyed world.

Abandoned Playground Draft:

The environment is grey, dark and full of cracks in the ground due to the drought.

Mountainside Draft:

It creates a very depressing and miserable atmosphere around the vibrant looking main characters.


Sprout:

Low poly sprout that appears after the final scene hug, is as bright and full of life as the two main characters. It has a striking, bold and eager character to it.


The Little Fox by Zplay Technology CO.:

Our group was inspired by a charming and alluring look of the character from The Little Fox game because, despite rather a blocky design, the fox still managed to feel delicate and tender.


Monument Valley by Ustwo Game:

In terms of the character environment, Monument Valley game was taken as our next animation inspiration for we absolutely adored this rectangular, yet elegant feel of the castles, which do not disrupt the harmony of the scene.
