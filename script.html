Iceboy and Firegirl

The world is destroyed because of human pollution. Everything around is grey and ashy. The cities are long gone, the earth is dry and there is no greenery. There are only two people left on the earth: the fire girl and the ice boy. One has emerged from a disrupting volcano and the other one from the icicle that ones fell from the sky.

They met accidentally while wandering the earth and became close friends. And although they were close friends, they never dared to touch each other because if they do, they both would disappear. However, one day, after looking back at the dried out and miserable world that they were living in, the friends made a decision. They decided to sacrifice themselves for the sake of life for they knew that together they are capable of curing this eternal drought. They hugged - for the first and last time, becoming water that sipped through the dry earth and helped the first sprout of the new life to appear.
